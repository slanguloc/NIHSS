# Privacy Policy — Zysquy

**Last updated:** [Add date when you publish]

## We do not collect your data

Zysquy **does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data** with us or with any third party. No account is required. We do not receive any information about your use of the app, your device, or any assessment or patient-related information.

## All information is stored locally on your device

All data created in the app stays **only on your device**. This includes:

- Your language preference (e.g. Spanish for patient prompts)
- Assessment history (e.g. scores and completion times from practice assessments)

Nothing is sent to the internet, to our servers, or to any other service for storage or processing.

## Stored data is encrypted for patient information safety

To protect any information you store in the app, **all data saved on your device is encrypted** before it is written to storage. Encryption is performed on-device using your device’s secure storage. This helps keep practice and assessment information private and secure, in line with patient information safety practices.

- Encryption keys are stored in your device’s secure enclave (Keychain) and never leave your device.
- Only the Zysquy app on the same device can decrypt the data.
- Deleting the app removes all stored and encrypted data from that device.

## Speech and microphone

When you use speech recognition (e.g. to capture or translate patient-style responses), processing may be handled by your device’s operating system (e.g. Apple’s on-device speech recognition where available). We do not send voice or transcript data to our own servers or to any third party. Any transcript or translation used in the app remains on your device and is stored only as described above (locally and encrypted).
