# Legal and use disclaimer — Zysquy

## Education only — not for clinical use

**Zysquy is for education and training only. It is not intended for clinical use and must not be used to make or support clinical decisions, diagnoses, or treatment.**

### Intended use

- **Learning and training** on the NIH Stroke Scale (NIHSS).
- **Educational support** when practicing communication with non-English speaking patients (e.g. Spanish) in a training or simulation setting.
- **Reference** for phrases and structure of the scale in an educational context.

### Not intended

- **Clinical use** — The app must not be used during actual patient care to guide or document NIHSS assessments.
- **Medical device** — This app is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, or manage any medical condition.
- **Substitute for training** — It does not replace formal NIHSS certification, institutional protocols, or clinical judgment.
